What is CPEF?

In the summer of 2012 a group of community members with ties to the Clark-Pleasant Community School Corporation (CPCSC) met to discuss the possibility of forming a Not for Profit Foundation to raise money for programs and projects benefitting the students and staff of CPCSC.  After a couple of meetings a Board of Directors was chosen and work began to establish the Clark-Pleasant Education Foundation (CPEF).  You can see our current Board of Directors here, or make a donation on our Donation Page.

We are here to encourage, recognize, reward and support creativity, innovation, achievement, and excellence within the students, teachers and administrators of the CPCSC. We strive to develop partnerships with individuals, families, community groups, local businesses, and philanthropies to enhance educational opportunities within CPCSC. We seek to inspire parents and alumni of CPCSC to participate in the learning process. The foundation works with teachers and administrators in order to enhance the quality of public education.

We hope that you will partner with us in this endeavor. We are asking for your time, your talent and your financial support. If you would like to make a tax deductible donation to the Clark-Pleasant Community School Corporation please visit our Donations page for more information.

This is an exciting time for us and a wonderful opportunity to enhance the lives of our students. We have lofty goals and grand expectations and we need a mighty team to see them all accomplished. Won’t you join us?
