Funds

The Clark-Pleasant Education Foundation (CPEF) is currently steward of many individual funds.Â Most funds were set up by individual donors or groups of donors who are passionate about specific causes, programs or projects.

Not to diminish the importance of these funds to CPEF, we've implemented them as options for our donars. Making financial contribution amongst the individual funds is simple. Please pick from one of the following five broad categories of charitable giving.

Donate Via Check

If you prefer making your donation via check, please make your check payable to the Clark-Pleasant Education Foundation and mail it to:

CPEF
50 Center Street,
Whiteland, IN 46184.

Please designate on your check if your donation is intended for a specific fund, program or project.
